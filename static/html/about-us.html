KAMPAI SAKE BOX'S MISSION

DEMOCRATIZE SAKE

BRING PEOPLE TOGETHER

LEAVE A LEGACY

In our modern world, Sake culture seems mysterious and inaccessible to most. That’s why Kampai Sake is here - we want to bring the beauty of Sake to everyone. By helping you fall in love with Sake and increasing its consumption outside of Japan, we hope to allow Sake brewers to continue to preserve this centuries-old art form. We kampai (cheers) to bring together new drinkers and Japanese Sake brewers.

With every box you receive, not only are you embarking on a Sake journey, but you are also helping those in need. Our philosophy is that business exists to support and contribute to communities. Let’s Kampai, have a blast and make a difference together!

KAMPAI SAKE BOX STORY


I was born and raised in Japan and originally moved to the US for school. I have deep ties with my native country and love learning about my own culture and tradition. This joy intensifies when I am able to share this passion with others. It is through this process that I fell in love with the beauty and exquisite craftsmanship of Sake making. Every Sake maker is an artist who creates unique types of Sake based on their region’s local traditions and climate. They unify their communities through celebration and tradition.

I do not consider myself a Sake expert, but I have always known to enjoy Sake fully by heart and yearned to introduce it to the world. I allow Sake makers to tell their stories as I connect them to people who will enjoy drinking their Sake. From my corner in sunny California, I am happy to do my part to cheer and support Japanese craftsmanship. Kampai to everyone!
